With adding PS5 and XBOX exclusives onto the Steam platform for PC players, I have designed these visualisations showing how these consoles and their games have shaped the entertainment industry. I have analysed data regarding popularity of consoles and their game ratings.
Here is a graph showing the popularity by Google searches, of a few trending gaming consoles on social media. This includes the Steam Deck console, which is yet to release in Australia. The data is normalised such that for example, between all countries, whichever has "PS5" as most searched term is given the benchmark 100, and all other countries are compared against it. The same applies for other consoles that can be viewed through the selection box.
